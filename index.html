Artificial Life and Digital Evolution

Digital Evolution employs computational methods to simulate the evolution and adaptation of digital organisms within a virtual environment. My focus was on using Digital Evolution to explore the intricate relationship between host organisms and parasites. Each organism bore a unique genome, evolving over time to optimize its goal attainment. The outcome of my experimentation illuminated an unexpected advantage: the presence of parasites fosters heightened competition, leading to unanticipated benefits for reproducer organisms. I expanded this study by modifying variables, allowing me to investigate ecosystem responses across diverse circumstances and extended timeframes.
